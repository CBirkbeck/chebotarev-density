Definition 1.3

The inertia group at \(\mathfrak {P}\) is

\[ I_\mathfrak {P}\; =\; \left\{ \sigma \in G \; \middle |\; \forall x\in \mathcal{O}_L,\; \sigma (x)\equiv x\pmod\mathfrak {P} \right\} . \]
